BIO
Gabby Gaston is a pianist, composer and sound designer living in Issaquah, Washington, primarily focused on audio for video games. In 2022 she graduated from DigiPen Institute of Technology with a bachelor's degree in Music and Sound Design.


Working with student teams, she produced the sound design and music for two games (Pogo Pirate and Ceramic Soul). Over the summer of 2021 she collaborated with Brass Band Northwest, composing a piece that was performed during their summer and fall concerts.


Currently, Gabby is an audio intern at valve for the summer. She is fueled by copious amounts of tea.
